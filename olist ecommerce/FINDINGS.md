### Key Findings

**Revenue Concentration Risk** 

Top 5 categories = 68% of revenue | Furniture alone = 18%

- Risk: Over-reliance on few categories
- Action: Expand underperforming categories through targeted marketing

**High Churn in Single-Purchase Customers**

75% of customers = one-time buyers | Only 19% repeat purchase rate

- Risk: Low customer lifetime value
- Action: Implement retention programs and loyalty rewards

**Payment Method Impacts Fulfillment**

Credit card = 94% completion rate vs Installment = 88%

- Implication: Installment plans have higher operational risk
- Action: Investigate fraud and payment processing issues; consider stricter verification

**Geographic Hotspots Underserved**

3 states = 52% of revenue | 18 states = <1% each

- Opportunity: Northern and Central-West regions severely underpenetrated
- Action: Test marketing campaigns in underserved states; expand seller networks

**Review Score Predicts Loyalty**

5-star reviews = 26% repeat rate vs 3-star or below = 8%

- Insight: Customer satisfaction directly drives revenue
- Action: Implement quality scoring; escalate negative reviews; create buyer protection
